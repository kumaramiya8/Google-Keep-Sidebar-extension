Quick Tips

  • 🖱️
    Right-Click Capture

    Select text on any webpage, right-click, and select "Send to Google Keep" to copy it instantly.

  • 📋
    Easy Paste

    After right-clicking, click anywhere in the Keep iframe and press Cmd+V (or Ctrl+V) to paste.

  • 🔑
    Sign In Required?

    If you see a login screen that won't load, click the button to sign in in a new tab, then refresh.
